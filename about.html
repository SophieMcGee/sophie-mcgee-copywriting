A LITTLE BIT ABOUT ME


Hello! I’m Sophie, let’s connect, collaborate and create great content!


From my base in County Donegal, Ireland, I provide a copywriting service to businesses across Ireland, the UK and even as far as America! With more than 10 years spent working as a marketing manager combined with a decade of copywriting experience, I have the skills needed to bridge the gap between strategy and creativity.


My mission is simple, to craft compelling copy through strategic, content-driven solutions that are tailored to suit every client.


In the vast and often challenging landscape of copywriting, finding a professional who not only understands the nuances of your industry but also aligns with your brand's voice can be a journey. I've been there, and it's this understanding that fuels my commitment to building genuine connections with each client.


A personalised approach


I pride myself on more than just delivering words; it's about understanding your business inside out. This personalised approach allows me to grasp your unique goals, ensuring that every piece of content reflects your brand identity and resonates with your audience.


An extension of your team


Think of me as a dedicated extension of your team, a reliable member of your marketing department, always on hand to provide quick, accurate, and strategically cohesive copy. Your success is my priority, and I'm here to ensure that your content not only meets but exceeds your expectations.


Extensive industry experience


With a background spanning everything from fintech and retail to property and healthcare, I've delivered content goals for businesses of all sizes. Whether you're launching a product, revamping your online presence, or telling your brand's story, my experience allows me to adapt and excel in any industry. Whether it's a captivating blog post, an engaging social media campaign, or a comprehensive website overhaul, I bring a strategic edge to every word, ensuring it aligns seamlessly with your broader marketing strategy.
